TOP Role Meaning

The Top lane role is a role where you are alone in the lane, you are in front of the enemy top laner fighting for control of the map. Top lane champions usually consist of Tank Champions, Bruiser Champions and sometimes Even Support Champions.So if you like champions that take no damage or champions that are mixed damage and defense top lane is a role that you will like.Also if you like fighting solo early and destroying the enemy top lane is a good match for you as there is not much objectives around top lane at early stages in the game.

Like all roles in the game top lane has a lot more to do than just fight the enemy, top laners must also keep the minion farm up they should ward the area of the enemy jungler also get ready to help you're jungler for objectives later on. The primary goal of a top laner is to get the enemy tower and open his part of the map and take control of top side of the map. Whomever takes control of his side of the map he wins his role that goes for all lanes not just top lane. In top lane you use two to three summoner spells depending on the champion you pick. Those summoner spells are Teleport, Ignite and Flash. If you have teleport then the best ways to use it early is if you fight and are low hp you recall and get back to lane using teleport that way you don't miss minions,the other way is to look for teleport opportunity to help you're other teammates in need most common bot lane fights and objectives like dragon.A good way yo use teleport is if you're fighting a strong enemy top laner who does not have teleport you play very aggressive early by fighting and letting the wave push to you're tower that way you can recall and get back to laner with full health and not miss a minion.

Team Fight Tanks

There are a lot of different champions you can pick in top lane, but the most common champions you play when you're with a team you know are Tank champions.
Tank champions don't really go off the meta because they are all ways reliable front liners to tank the enemy carrys damage and protect you're damage dealers. I will give you some recommendations for Tank champions.

1. Ornn
. Ornn is a very reliable Tank he has a lot of crowd control which consist of a lot of knock ups and slows. Ornn also has a shield on is W combine that with how big of a tank he becomes later on he is very hard to kill. Plus his abilities do hp% damage so he has a lot of potential to kill in lane,but Ornn does need more practice to get good with his abilities as some of them are hard to hit.
2. Malphite
. Malphite like Ornn is really tanky he also has a shield that comes of his passive. Unlike Ornn his abilities are much more easy to land and need practically no skill except his ultimate that is a skill shot. Malphite in lane is very good at sustain with his passive shield and poke with his abilities. Once you bring you're enemies hp down he is very good at chasing and killing them with his Q. A goood Malphite on a teamfight will use his ultimate to turn the fight in his allies favor.
3. Shen .
Shen really shines in early fights because of his ultimate where he channels a teleports to a allie anywhere in the map and gives a big shield to protect them. This can turn the tide of the fight really fast in the favor of you're team in small teamfights like in bot lane 2v2 or mid lane. You can help you're team win early fights and become more feed in the game. Late game Shen provides stuns, slows and a lot of tankines, with his W that makes him and his team immune to auto attack damage in a small area.

Those were some of the strongest tanks right know but there is a lot more of them you can check them out in the game and see which ones you like.

Solo Carry Champions

Solo carry champions we usually call those bruisers with a lot of damage while still being tanky. The difference from tanky champions is that bruisers have less crowd control and are less tanky, but thats not a problem because they make up with the damage they deal. Solo carry champions are champions you play when you don't play with a team you know and like to have matters more in you're hands. These kind of champions require a lot of skill to play successfully and win. You win you're lane hard then you take you're lead around the map and take you're team forward. Some of the best solo carry champions for top are:

1. Fiora .
Fiora is one of the strongest duelist champions with a lot of outplay potential. Few champions can get away from Fiora once she engages. She has a lot of damage with the potential to one shot squishy champions with a swift combo of abilities. Late game almost no one can beat Fiora in a 1v1 scenario. After she kills a enemy with her ultimate she leaves a mark on the ground that heals allies that stand on top of it. She is one of the best split push champions as she has good escape and good 1v2 or 1v3 potential, with a W that used correctly can reflect a crowd control ability to the enemies.
2. Jax .
Jax is a very strong champion with a lot of mobility with his low cd Q and a lot of basic attack damage with his W and ultimate passive. He has a stun on his E that he channels while moving and while he channels he takes no damage from auto attack. He is very squishy early but he scales a lot late game becoming more tanky and a lot more damage with power to solo teamfights and splitpush.
3. Darius .
Darius is very strong early with a lot of damage and sustain. Few to none champions can kill him early if played correctly. Darius doesn't have much mobility but he makes up with a Q that heals a W that slows and a E that pulls enemies close to him. His passive once stacked gives him almost unfair damage and his ultimate with a stacked passive does a huge amount of damage, if you kill someone with you're ultimate it resets and you can use it again. As a Darius you look to win you're lane hard get ahead early and destroy teamfights mid game.
4. Renekton .
Renekton is similar in the way you win a game like Darius, getting ahead early and destroying teamfights midgame . Unlike Darius he has a mobility and also has a Stun on his W. He also has a lot of sustain with his passive and Q. It is really hard to get away from a Renekton as he can dash 2 times with his E if he hits it once he can cast it again. His ultimate makes him bigger increasing his auto attack range and the range of his abilities plus he gains a aura around him that damages enemies close to him, once Renekton activates his ultimate he becomes a real monster on the rift.

There are a lot of different solo carry champions these were just some of the best you can look at others and play what you most enjoy
