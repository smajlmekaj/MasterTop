Early Levels Wave Management

Wave management is an important role for any lane. For top lane you need to be very carful with you're Wave because miss positioning it can lead to losing the lane. There are three important wave management styles first is freezing then comes slow pushing and fast pushing. Freezing is good early if you're with a champion that is a lane bully it can help make you're enemy lose minions gold and XP by freezing the wave in front of you're tower. Freezing is done when the enemy minion wave has 4 minions more then you're wave and last hitting the enemy minions by not letting them crash in you're tower. Freezing is also good when you're in a losing champion matchup that way you can be safe under you're tower while farming. Slow pushing in the early game is a good way to build a huge allie minion wave and crashing it in the enemy tower, its a good way to set up dives make pressure and baiting. Slow pushing is done by killing the caster minions in the wave and leaving the melee minions to last hit. Fast pushing is a good way to punish you're enemy when you win a trade and they recall or when you kill them. It will get the allie minions to die to the enemy tower and not let the enemy champion farm them. Fast pushing can be done by killing the minions as fast as possible with abilities and not just normal attacks.

When Ahead

When ahead in top lane its good to focus on getting objectives and roaming around the map. If you can get the tower thats the first thing you should be focused on by making slow pushes and crashing big waves in the tower. If not you should kill the minions as fast as possible and look for herald ganks mid or helping you're jungler.

When Behind

When behind you should focus on farming and staying alive best way to do that is to do that is by freezing the way in front of you're tower and farming. Freezing the wave also gives opportunities for ganks as the enemy laner must walk deep on the lane to get farm and not get behind, if you can get a kill or two with ganks that will make a huge difference for you. Also if you're behind you should build you're items with tank stats as that will be more beneficial for the team.

Tank Team Fights

As a tank champion in team fights you are the front line of defence for you're team. You should be focused to not let any enemy champion break through you and attack the back line. Tank champions also have the best engages because the have a lot of crowd control abilities, so if you're team is close look for a pick best ones being the damage dealers of the enemy team. Use you're abilities to cancel the counter attack or engages of the enemy tanks or assassin's, then just stay in front so they cant attack the back line.

Fighters Team Fights

Fighter champions have a lot of kill potential for the enemy squishy champions, if you can pick one when they are out of place that will be a great engage that you're team can follow on. As a fighter champion you should focus on getting past the tanks and killing the back line as fast as possible with you're high damage and after disengage and survive with you're medium tankines until you're abilities come back on.
A very effective strategy for some fighter champions is split pushing, you do that by getting far ahead in the lane phase and focusing on destroying the enemy towers and inhibitors. Split pushing means pushing alone and trying to either get the towers or make the enemy bring two or more champions to stop you that way you leave you're team in a advantageous situation of 4v3 team fights.
